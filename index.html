THE WITCHER GAMES

Based on the book series of the same name by polish writer Andrzej Sapkowski

01

THE WITCHER

The story takes place in a medieval fantasy world and follows Geralt of Rivia, one of a few traveling monster hunters who have supernatural powers, known as Witchers. It was released in 2007 to positive reviews from critics and received an aggregate score of 81/100 on Metacritic.

02

THE WITCHER 2: Assassins of Kings

The fantasy world in which the adventures take place owes much to Polish history and Slavic mythology. The game was both a critical and commercial success, selling over 8 million copies by September 2014.

03

THE WITCHER 3: Wild Hunt

Upon its release, The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt became an instant classic, claiming over 250 Game of the Year awards. You can enjoy this huge, over 100-hour long, open-world adventure along with both its story-driven expansions worth an extra 50 hours of gameplay.
